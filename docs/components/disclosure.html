Disclosure

Selector
details > summary

The disclosure (also known as accordion or collapsible) is a native HTML widget that can show and hide content when the disclosure button is toggled. The summary should be the first child of the details element. Any other elements in the details will be the content that gets shown and hidden. They can be used on their own or in a group, in which case, surround the disclosures with a <div class="dislosure-group" /> to group them visually.

National Mango Recognition The Mango is the national fruit of Pakistan, India, and the Philippines.
State Mango Recognition The Mango is the official fruit of Chandigarh and Maharashtra.
Personal Mango Recognition I just find them tasty.
A disclosure on its own Hello!

To have a disclosure that is open by default, add the open attribute to the details element.
