Miami Mangos

Growing up, I had a mango tree. So did some of my friends. Late summer rained endless mangos onto my backyard and mango vendors would line some of the roads nearby. A visit to a friend's house could start with abuela's mango smoothie and end with mango smiles.

Design

MangoUI started as an experiment to transfer these experiences to a website. The colors are lifted directly from the fruit. Controls depress slightly when clicked, similar to pressing a ripe mango. The fonts, icons, and components are designed to only be round.
